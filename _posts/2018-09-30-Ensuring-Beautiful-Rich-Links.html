Ensuring Beautiful Rich Links

Tech Talks

Titles

  • Keep titles unique and informative
  • Avoid duplicating the site name
  • JavaScript is not run, so don’t generate tags dynamically

Icons

Images

  • Only use og:image for interesting images (og = open graph)
  • Generic fallback images should be icons instead
  • Images should not include text
  • Specify an icon even if you also have an image

Videos

  • Icon, image, and video together are limited to total of 10MB
  • Use directly-referenced video files for autoplay
  • Avoid embedded video that requires HTML or plugins
